# 📖 koryomi - Manage and read your manga library

Koryomi serves as a single home for your manga and manhwa collection. It organizes your files, manages downloads, and provides a clean reader interface for your devices. You replace multiple complex services with one tool.

## 🛠 Features

*   **Integrated Library:** Store and view manga and manhwa in one place.
*   **Automated Tracking:** Follow your favorite series and download new chapters as they release.
*   **Offline Access:** Save files to your computer for reading without an internet connection.
*   **Web Interface:** Use your browser to read comics on any screen.
*   **PWA Support:** Install the reader as an app on your desktop or phone.

## 📋 System Requirements

*   **Operating System:** Windows 10 or 11.
*   **Storage:** At least 2GB of free disk space for your collection.
*   **Memory:** 4GB of RAM is sufficient for smooth operation.
*   **Network:** An active internet connection for downloading new chapters.

## ⚙️ Initial Setup

When you open Koryomi for the first time, the program asks for a folder location. Choose a folder where you want to store your manga files. This folder functions as your main library.

After selecting the folder, Koryomi shows the main dashboard. You can add your first series by clicking the plus sign in the corner. Paste the URL of the manga you want to track or search through the built-in library options.

The application checks for new chapters at set intervals. You can change these settings in the preferences menu.

## 📱 Using the Reader

Koryomi provides an interface for reading your files. Click on any cover in your library to see the list of chapters. Select a chapter to open the reader.

*   **Navigation:** Use your arrow keys or click the sides of the screen to turn pages.
*   **Layout:** Adjust settings in the bottom menu to switch between single-page and double-page views.
*   **Reading Mode:** Enable webtoon mode for long-strip comics to scroll vertically.

## 🌐 Setting up the Web Reader

You can access your library from another device, such as a tablet or phone.

1. Open Koryomi on your Windows computer.
2. Go to the Settings tab and find the Network section.
3. Note the local address provided by the application.
4. Type this address into your browser on another device connected to the same home network.
5. Bookmark this page to your home screen to use Koryomi like a standard app.

## 🔧 Frequently Asked Questions

**Does Koryomi work offline?**
Yes. Once you download chapters to your library, you can read them without an internet connection.

**Can I import my existing library?**
Yes. When you set your library folder, move your existing files into that directory. The program detects new files and adds them to your list.

**How do I update to the newest version?**
Koryomi notifies you when an update exists. Click the notification to download and install the latest patches. This keeps your library secure and adds new features.

**What formats does the reader support?**
Koryomi supports common comic formats like CBZ, CBR, and PDF.

## 🛡 Security and Privacy

Koryomi runs locally on your machine. Your library data stays on your hard drive. No third-party server tracks your reading habits or stores your personal file information. Since you host the application, you maintain full control over your media.

If you encounter issues, check the log file located in your application data directory. This file tracks errors and helps you identify why a download or library sync failed.

Keep your file permissions set to private if you share your computer with other users. This ensures only you can access your manga content.

## 🚀 Troubleshooting

If Koryomi fails to download a chapter, verify your internet connection. Check that your storage folder has space remaining. Most download issues stem from restricted network access or full hard drives.

If the application logs you out, restart the process. If problems persist, clear your browser cache specifically for the Koryomi local address.

Regularly backup your library folder. Copy your manga directory to an external drive or cloud storage to prevent data loss if your computer fails.

Koryomi aims to provide a reliable way to manage digital comics. Stick to the suggested steps for the best experience. The application architecture allows for high performance without high resource usage.

Maintain your library by removing old files occasionally. Use the built-in storage management tool to show which series occupy the most space. This keeps your folder tidy and makes browsing faster for your device.

Enjoy your reading experience with this self-hosted solution. The project relies on community feedback to improve. Report bugs through the issue tracker on the official repository.
